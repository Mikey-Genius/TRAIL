How can we help you

Top 10 Frequently asked questions(FAQ)
  • What is Recharge.com?

    • At Online Recharge you can recharge phone credit internationally and 24/7. You can recharge your own credit or that of your friends and family abroad.

  • How do I recharge phone credit?

    • It’s super easy! Just follow these steps and you’ll be ready in no-time. Select carrier and product: let us know which product you’re going to buy - Fill in your information: fill in your email address and/or the recipient’s phone number - Pay and receive: after your payment the order will be sent immediately

  • To which countries can I make international recharges?

    • At this moment, you can recharge to more than 140 countries worldwide. That’s right - over 140 countries! Because we’re always expanding, we’ll add even more countries to our product range, making things even easier for you.

  • What can I do if I accidentally recharged the wrong number?

    • When you find out you've entered the wrong number, it's always possible to contact Customer Care, but keep in mind that in most cases neither they can cancel your order anymore. In the end you are responsible for the number you enter.

  • Can my phone credit be used for mobile data?

    • We take care of your phone credit, but it is up to your carrier to decide what you can use this credit for. Many carriers allow you to use the credit for mobile data as well, but with some you can’t. If you want to know whether your carrier allows you to use your credit for mobile data, please contact your carrier.

  • Can I refund my order?

    • When a direct recharge already succeeded or you already received the recharge code, it unfortunately is not possible to cancel the order and request a refund.

  • Is it possible to get an invoice from my order?

    • We’ll send an order confirmation to the email address you filled in while placing your order. Unfortunately, our system is not programmed to give out invoices. Direct recharges and recharge codes are excluded from tax, so if you’re a business customer you don’t have to worry about that. You can find the essential details about your order in the order confirmation you’ve received by email. Can’t find the order confirmation? Please let us know, so we can send it to you again.

  • Which payment methods are available?

    • That all depends on from which country you recharge. During the order process we clearly indicate which payment methods are accepted, including Mastercard and VISA. You can find an overview of the available payment methods on our payment methods page.

  • Why don’t I get to see all the payment methods?

    • We always show you the universal payment methods such as VISA and Mastercard. Next to these we offer local payment methods, such as iDeal and Carte Bancaire. The amount of credit you’re ordering also plays a role. Every payment method has its own minimum and maximum amount. Sometimes the currency you’ve selected is not supported by every payment method. These factors can contribute to you not getting to see all the payment methods. Fortunately, there are still many other payment methods you can choose from.

  • Can I make an account on Online Recharge?

    • Sure you can! If you register using your email address and a password, you can check your order history and find out the status of your current orders. Moreover, you can save your personal information, like your carrier and phone number, so your next order will go even faster.
